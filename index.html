FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about dental implants.

What are All-on-4 dental implants?

All-on-4 is a revolutionary technique that uses just four dental implants to support a full arch of teeth. It's designed for patients who need full-arch restoration and can often be completed in a single day.

Am I a candidate for dental implants?

Most adults with missing teeth are candidates. During your free consultation, we'll perform a 3D CT scan to evaluate your bone structure and create a personalized treatment plan.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. We back our implants with a lifetime warranty for your peace of mind.

Is the procedure painful?

We use advanced sedation techniques to ensure your comfort. Most patients report minimal discomfort and are pleasantly surprised by how easy the recovery is.

What if I have severe bone loss?

We specialize in complex cases. Our zygomatic implants are designed specifically for patients with severe bone loss who have been told they're not candidates elsewhere.

How much do dental implants cost?

Costs vary based on your specific needs. We offer free consultations and flexible financing with 0% interest options to make treatment accessible.
